# 📊 Business Insights — Customer Churn Analysis

## 🧠 Executive Summary

This project analyzes customer churn behavior using machine learning and identifies high-risk customers likely to leave. It also quantifies the financial impact of churn and provides actionable strategies to reduce revenue loss.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

### 1. High Revenue at Risk

* ₹23.22K annual revenue is at risk from high-probability churn customers
* Majority of this risk comes from customers on month-to-month contracts

👉 **Action:** Encourage long-term contracts through discounts and loyalty benefits

---

### 2. Retention Opportunity

* Total high-risk customers identified: **22**
* Retaining just **20%** of these customers can save approximately **₹4.6K**

👉 **Action:** Focus retention efforts on top-risk customers with personalized offers

---

### 3. Contract Type Drives Churn

* Month-to-month customers have the highest churn rate
* Long-term contracts (1-year, 2-year) show significantly lower churn

👉 **Action:** Promote yearly and 2-year plans

---

### 4. Service Factors Impact Churn

* Customers without tech support show significantly higher churn
* Fiber optic users also exhibit higher churn behavior

👉 **Action:** Improve service quality and provide bundled support plans

---

### 5. Payment Method Behavior

* Customers using electronic check have the highest churn rate

👉 **Action:** Incentivize automatic payment methods like credit card or bank transfer

---

## 💰 Business Impact

* Identifying high-risk customers enables proactive retention strategies
* Reducing churn directly protects recurring revenue
* Data-driven targeting improves marketing efficiency and ROI

---

## 🎯 Retention Strategy

### 🔴 High Risk Customers

* Immediate outreach (call within 7 days)
* Offer discounts or service upgrades

---

### 🟡 Medium Risk Customers

* Targeted email campaigns
* Bundle offers (internet + support services)

---

### 🔵 Low Risk Customers

* No immediate action required
* Maintain engagement through regular communication

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how machine learning combined with business analytics can identify churn risks, quantify revenue impact, and guide strategic decision-making to improve customer retention.
